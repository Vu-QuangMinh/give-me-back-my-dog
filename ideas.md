# Character's special abilities
## Sonny
### Bomb 
Can be used once a round. Deal 2 damage, AOE 1. Cast range: 1. Has 1 HP. Will explode when attacked. Does not draw enemies aggro but does block movement. Sonny attack will push it first then it will explode. Actually Sonny attack always push first and damage second. This can be damage and bounced of by projectiles like normal.
Possible upgrades for bomb: Not yet implemented
- Sentient bomb: require more battery. Can move and self detonate
- Taunting bomb: draw aggro
- Poison bomb: Deal 1 poison damage per turn until target is killed. Poison is stackable.
- Mine: no longer block movement. Press E to detonate (free action)
- Grenade: + 2 cast range. Explode immediately (if you have sentinet bomb, it becomes homing rocket, + 5 cast range instead and +1 area of effect)
- Bomb bomb: upon exploding, spawn another identical bomb on the same tile but without the bomb bomb modification.
- More bomb: +1 bomb per round
- Push bomb: Bomb also push 2
- Acid bomb: affected enemies receive twice the damage for until the end of Player's next round (does not stack)

Can be used twice a turn. Infinite range. When use fire a hook bit (silver) and a rope to the target (brown rope line). Dealing 1 damage (to enemies, no damage to Sonny) and pull the target next to you (to the best fitting hex) or pull you next to the target if the target is immovable (some enemies will be immovable, walls and columns are considered immovable)
